About

Ideal for the budding podcaster or the seasoned pro, our Podcast Room is designed to elevate your auditory creations to new heights. With the same level of flexibility you've come to appreciate from Yellow Desk, you can hire our Podcast Room by the hour, or for a half or full day, based on your needs.


The Podcast Room is equipped with state-of-the-art recording equipment, ensuring that your audio content is crisp, clear, and professional. Along with the technical aspects, we've created a comfortable and inspiring environment that nurtures your creativity and keeps the conversation flowing.


Hiring our Podcast Room also grants you 24/7 access to all Yellow Desk facilities. Take a break in our well-stocked kitchen or grab a drink from the bar to loosen up before your recording session. At Yellow Desk, all you need to bring is your voice and your ideas - we'll take care of the rest.
